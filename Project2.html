10

Principles for

Good Design


1

communicates well

Design tells the viewer what the function of the design is just by looking at. The viewer does not get confused while looking at the product, the product should automatically say what it is.


2

is simple

Design should be easy and straightfoward for what it's used for. The product is not filled with many uncessary essentials.


3

is functionable

Design work should be used without any issues. Focuses more on functionability rather than the appearance itself.



4

is an eyecatcher

The design work catches the eye of the viewer and making the viewer want to buy it.


5

is emotional

We start to get attached to the design work itself, an example could be a wallet, we take our wallet everywhere and use it for many years, so the design work sticks with us.

6

is sustainable

Design work would last for a long time for the user to use without any problems.

7

is useful

Design works in many ways not just functional but also aestheticaly, it helps keep away any problems the product has.


8

is convenient

Design product is necessary on certain occasions, usually when someone needs to use or wear a design product, they can count on the product itself.


9

is honest

Design product stays consistent and works functionally with what it promises to do, it doesn not try to make the product more powerful or innovative than what it already is.

10

is fun

The whole design works functionally, it's neat, and has an aesthetic feel to it. You are pleased to use the design work because of how it looks and how it works.
